Educational Background

I studied at Anestar Bahati High School, later joining Zetech University in 2016 to study IT. I was really excited to join uni, not knowing the joke that tech studies is in Kenya. I studied till the last semester of my 2nd year, dropping out before the next semester.

It was then that a friend mentioned Moringa school, and I can proudly say and reaffirm joining it is the best decision I've ever made. I firmly believe the combined effort of Moringa School and I will enable me become the software developer I've always wanted to be.
